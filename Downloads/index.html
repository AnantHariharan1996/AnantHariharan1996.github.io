Love Wave Phase Velocity Maps of the United States

We have calculated Love Wave phase Velocity maps of the Continental United States at periods between 35-75 s. These are the first Love wave phase velocity maps from Earthquakes that span the entirety of USArray. They constrain structure within the lithosphere and uppermost asthenosphere. These results are described in Hariharan & Dalton, 2022. Click here to go to the repository containing the maps.
